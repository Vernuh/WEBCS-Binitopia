BINI MIKHA

Name: Mikhaila Janna Dimenmea Lim

Stage name: Mikha

Born: November 8, 2003

Birthplace: Cebu, Philippines

Education: Born in Cebu City, moved to Sta. Rosa, Laguna where she moved again to Cavite and then to San Juan / Quezon City. She attended numerous school activities where she was an athlete, dancer, majorette, cheerleading squad, and also a campus writer. She is part of her schooling (extracurriculars) in the Philippines, but decided to become a trainee in 2019 while she was still in school. She managed her high school education with training in singing, dancing, etc.

Family: She has two older siblings. Mikha is a Chinese Filipina.

Early Activities:

  • Crew up singing casually with her dad via karaoke and she's had a bild on this with more of fun, not competitions.
  • In high school, she was a cheerleader, which fueled her team dancing, performing tumbling, and build her love on gymnastics and cheerleading.
  • Auditioned for the Star Hunt auditions in Quezon City in 2018, was selected (out of many aspirants) to attend the 2-day boot camp, and after formally became a trainee at Star Hunt Academy in early 2019.

Position within BINI: Main Rapper, Lead Dancer, Visual of BINI

Media & Other Activities:

  • Played volleyball as a setter in the Star Magic All-Star Game: Funpamilya Edition, where even Best Setter and was selected for the Mythical Six.
  • Played Elle Luna in *He's Into Her Season 2* (joined/replaced previous actress).
  • Guest Judge on *Drag Den PH* (Season 2) in early 2024 alongside other BINI members.
  • Appeared in vlog / guest features e.g. Baddie-Bot series with Denise Julia.

Interests, Personality & Lifestyle:

  • Known for her signature red hair. Mikha has commented publicly about how it helps her stand out, what it means for her identity, and the importance of self-love that goes with maintaining the hair.
  • Interested in high-fashion accessory coverage e.g. she has been photographed using luxury bags like Fendi or Louis Vuitton, and her style is often the subject of fashion write-ups.
  • Participated in charitable / outreach work e.g. the *Anak ng Diyos Foundation*, providing assistance with children at Tondo, Manila through games, school supplies and gifts.
  • Participation in *Star Magic All-Star Games* sports: awards: Best Setter, and was captain of the Lady Setters, won Best Setter etc.
  • Public interactions: responded to criticisms about her acting and school/college roles with good grace, asked for kindness and understanding.
  • She's spoken about the challenge of being in a prominent group, that it's hard to sleep sometimes, thinking a lot, balancing expectations.

"Dreams will only stay dreams until you act upon it."
