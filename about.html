ALL ABOUT BINI

About Us

This website was created to celebrate and support BINI, the nation's girl group, while showcasing the creativity and pride of the Filipino people. It exists as a space where fans can come together to enjoy music, share fandom experiences, and highlight the talent that makes Filipinos shine. Beyond updates and fan activities, the site's purpose is to introduce both local and international audiences to the beauty of P-Pop and Filipino culture. It is more than a fan page. It's a community and a universe of music, joy, and national pride.

HISTORY OF BINI

Formation and Training

BINI is an eight-member P-pop girl group formed through ABS-CBN's Star Hunt Academy, which followed a Korean-style training system. The members underwent intensive vocal, dance, language, and personality development training for years, leading up to their pre-debut single "Da Coconut Nut" in 2020. They officially debuted on June 11, 2021, with "Born to Win," representing the strength and elegance of modern Filipinas.

Pre-Debut Activities

BINI originated from a group of trainees at Star Hunt Academy, the talent division of ABS-CBN. The members underwent training from 2018 to 2020, guided by a diverse array of coaches, including local talent such as Kitchy Molina and Korean instructors from MU Doctor Academy. Their debut performance as SHA trainees took place during the PBB Otso online pre-show in August 2019, which was followed by their inaugural mall show in Taguig.

Debut and Mainstream Success

BINI officially made their debut with the single "Born To Win" on June 11, 2021. The group garnered viral acclaim with their 2023 hit "Pantropiko," which not only topped various charts but also inspired a multitude of dance challenges on platforms such as TikTok. By 2024, both "Pantropiko" and "Salamin, Salamin" occupied the #1 and #2 positions on the Billboard Philippines charts, solidifying BINI's status as one of the preeminent female artists in the Philippines.

Current Status

The group has achieved a remarkable milestone by selling out their "BINIverse" World Tour, with tickets for their concert at the New Frontier Theater disappearing in under two hours, prompting the addition of more show dates. BINI has garnered international acclaim, as their music and performances resonate with audiences worldwide. In September 2025, the Filipino girl group is scheduled to perform at the inaugural Filipino Music Awards (FMAs) and is also preparing for a year-end concert at the Philippine Arena in November 2025, following the success of their earlier "BINIverse World Tour". Additionally, they will host a fan meet in Cagayan de Oro on September 28, 2025, and are teasing new music, with plans to release additional songs as they continue their activities throughout the year.

Beyond music, this site is about celebrating Filipino pride. BINI's journey inspires us to support local talent, embrace our culture, and share it with the world. Whether you're a BLOOM or a new listener, you are welcome here.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Concert and touring milestones

The group made history in 2024 with their Biniverse concert series, achieving sell-out performances across multiple dates at the New Frontier Theater and subsequently at the Araneta Coliseum—an unprecedented accomplishment for a Filipino girl group.

Bini made history as the first Filipino act to sell out the 55,000-seat Philippine Arena during their Valentine's Day performance in February 2025, marking the launch of their Biniverse World Tour.The group made history in 2024 with their Biniverse concert series, achieving sell-out performances across multiple dates at the New Frontier Theater and subsequently at the Araneta Coliseum—an unprecedented accomplishment for a Filipino girl group. The success of these concerts not only showcased their rapidly growing fanbase but also solidified their status as one of the country’s top live performers.

BINI continued to break records in 2025 when they became the first Filipino act to sell out the 55,000-seat Philippine Arena during their special Valentine’s Day performance, marking the grand launch of their Biniverse World Tour. This monumental event highlighted their evolution from rising P-pop stars to internationally recognized performers.

Following the Philippine Arena concert, the group brought their tour to key cities across Asia, the Middle East, and North America, performing in major venues and attracting diverse audiences. Their shows were praised for their high-energy choreography, powerful vocals, and immersive production, further cementing BINI’s reputation as global ambassadors of P-pop.

Music and streaming records

Viral Chart-Toppers

In 2024, the group topped the Billboard Philippines Songs chart with their viral hits "Pantropiko" and "Salamin, Salamin," the first P-pop group to do so.

Spotify Records

  • First Filipino act to enter the Spotify Top Artists Global chart (2024).
  • First Filipino act to top the Spotify Philippines' Daily Top Artists chart (2024).
  • First Filipino group to surpass 6 million monthly Spotify listeners.
  • First female OPM artist to break the 7 million monthly listener mark on Spotify.
  • Surpassed one billion total streams on Spotify in 2025.

YouTube Success

The music videos for "Salamin, Salamin" and "Pantropiko" achieved the top two positions on YouTube Philippines' music charts in 2024, ranking #1 and #2, respectively. They continue to perform exceptionally well on the platform, maintaining the number one spot to this day.
