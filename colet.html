BINI COLET

Name: Maria Nicolette Florenosos Vergara

Stage name: Colet

Born: September 14, 2001

Birthplace: Tagbilaran City, Bohol, Philippines

Education: She studied vocals under the Special Program for the Arts at Dr. Cecilio Putong National High School, graduated senior high as a scholar at Japan-Philippine Institute of Technology in 2022, and is now pursuing Communications at Tames International School.

Family: She comes from a big family (six siblings). Her father is a singer and drummer.

Early Activities:

  • Her father was a singer and drummer, so she grew up exposed to music. This encouraged her to pursue performing.
  • She joined the Special Program for the Arts (SPA) in high school at Dr. Cecilio Putong National High School where she majored in vocals. This honed her skills in singing and performing at a young age.
  • Competitions & performances: She actively joined singing contests, dance performances, and school events in Bohol, showing her versatility as a performer.

Position within BINI: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer, Lead Rapper.

Songwriting:

Co-writer of BINI songs such as "Golden Arrows," "Kinikilig," and "Karera". She also solely wrote the song "B".

Recognition & Other Ventures:

  • On July 1, 2024, she received a Plaque of Recognition from the city government of Tagbilaran for her dedication to music.
  • Her musical ability has received praise; for example, critics and publications note her strong vocal technique, her ability to handle rap + vocals + dance.

Interests, Personality & Lifestyle:

  • Hobbies: singing, dancing, reading books.
  • Color preference: Black is her favorite color.
  • Nicknames: Mani, Anger, Colet.
  • Motto / Philosophy: "It's okay not to be okay."

"I am strong-willed and very passionate about my and our career. I value hard work and always try to seek perfection. This trait has served me well. I really want our group to do great, so I always try to communicate with the members about our weaknesses as individuals and as a group. I do not only want to be the best, I want others to be the best too. We are a team, and no one should be left behind."
